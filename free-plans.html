A bottle cap or any rounded lid can be used to mark the radius of the cuts. In the bottom left are the 2 controller boxes to be attached to the sides of the cocktail table base. The bottom right box has the measurements for the front controller box to be attached to the front of the cocktail table base. For the top of the cocktail table, in the lower middle, the same bottle cap or rounded lid can be used for the radiuses. The top should fit a monitor up to 22 inches. Use a piece of paper and draw an outline of the screen of your monitor, cut it out, and use that as a guide to measure where to make the center hole for your monitor.
